# PharmaTrack+

## Intelligent Medicine Management

PharmaTrack+ is an AI-powered platform designed to streamline healthcare needs by providing medicine verification, alternative suggestions, emergency services, and medication reminders.

---

## Features

- **Verify Medicine:** Scan QR codes to instantly verify the authenticity of medicines.
- **Find Alternatives:** Discover cost-effective generic alternatives with the same composition and effectiveness.
- **Emergency Mode:** Quick access to nearby hospitals and emergency medical services.
- **Medicine Reminders:** Set up personalized medication schedules and receive smart reminders.

---

## How It Works

1. **Home:** Overview of services and navigation.
2. **Verify:** Scan or enter medicine details to check authenticity.
3. **Alternatives:** Search for generic or alternative medicines.
4. **Emergency:** Locate nearby hospitals and emergency contacts.
5. **Reminders:** Set and manage medication schedules.

---

## Getting Started

1. Clone or download the repository.
2. Open `index.html` in your browser to explore the platform.
